Trial snapshots

What the data means for your crops.

Use these highlights to brief crews or customers on how frass behaves in the field.

Vegetables

Split-applying 300 lbs/acre pre-plant plus a sidedress at first bloom delivered 11% more marketable tomatoes in the UF trial.

Hydroponics

Frass-based compost teas reduced Pythium incidence in leafy green troughs without clogging emitters when filtered to 200 microns.

Turf & sports

Combining frass with hollow-tine aeration increased infiltration 0.4 in/hr over urea controls in the Scientific Reports study.

Compost operations

Adding 1% frass by weight shortened thermophilic phases by 4 days and reduced odor complaints in municipal composting trials.
